Frosty the Fridge

Frosty the Fridge has many features. His rapidly blinking eyes prevent him from missing anything. His four magnets on his body gives him ways to hold things on his body. His mouth, born from evoultion, grants him the ability to speak, preventing those who want to steal his riches. The coloring of his body signifies his uniqueness. The handle on his body opens only to those he deems worthy.
